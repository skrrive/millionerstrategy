Refund Policy

Last updated: July 27, 2025

All Subscriptions & Lifetime Purchases

Due to the digital nature of our products and the instant access provided to our proprietary indicators upon purchase, we maintain a strict no-refund policy.

Once a purchase has been made and the software has been delivered, we cannot issue a refund. This policy is in place to protect our intellectual property and prevent abuse of our systems. We encourage you to review all information on our website and ask any questions before making a purchase.

Subscription Cancellations

All recurring subscriptions (e.g., Monthly, 3-Months) can be cancelled at any time. It is your responsibility to manage your account and cancel your subscription through your Whop dashboard before the next billing cycle if you do not wish to continue. No refunds will be issued for partial subscription periods.

Exceptions

An exception may be made for duplicate payments due to a verifiable system error. If you believe you have been charged twice for the same billing period, please contact our support at support@millionerstrategy.com with proof of both transactions.
